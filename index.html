How I Work

My SEO Process

Same repeatable framework across all 50+ client projects. No guesswork, no shortcuts.

01

Discovery & Audit

I start by understanding your business, your competitors, and your current SEO health. Every technical issue, every content gap, and every quick win gets documented before a single change is made.

02

Keyword Strategy

I map high-intent, high-value keywords to specific pages on your site. The goal is always buyer intent — finding people who are already looking for what you offer.

03

On-Page & Technical Optimization

I fix what's broken — site speed, Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, internal linking, meta tags, schema markup. Then I optimize your content to match what Google actually wants to rank.

04

Link Building

Manual outreach to earn quality backlinks from sites that actually matter in your industry. This is the slow part — but it's what separates rankings that last from rankings that disappear overnight.

05

Monthly Reporting & Refinement

Every month you get a clear report — what moved, what didn't, why, and what I'm doing next. I adjust the strategy based on real data, not assumptions.
